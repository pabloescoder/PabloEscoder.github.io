How It is DoNe

First, our design team spends hours to create the perfect design. Functional, robust and aesthetic.

Then, we import the best materials from various parts of the world because quality, matters.

Different parts of the watch are made in different workstations specialized for that purpose.

Once cut, the parts are washed and checked by quality control so that every part of your watch is perfect.

Next, our highly specialized watchmaker assembles all the parts together and creates a stunning Cronus.

Lastly the Cronus is checked on different vibrations to ensure that it works in every condition.
